About Me

Travel & Food is a travel blog written to inspire and empower travelers, share travel stories and introduce you to the cuisines of different countries.

Hello and welcome to my blog! My name is Vladislav. A little about me - I love to travel and photograph everything beautiful, I love delicious and beautiful food, and I'm also a beginner developer.

I always travel with my wife Anastasia. Together we explore the world, get acquainted with the culture of other countries, take many beautiful photographs to share it with you.

The greatest aspect of traveling is the ability to witness all elements of life come together into a kind of foreshortened timeline. It's as if we are simultaneously time traveling through our past and future. Secured by its focal present, collectively combining all lessons into our own being at each point in time and in each present moment.

Countries we visited:

Egypt, Spain, Turkey, UAE, Qatar, Philippines, Thailand, Malaysia, Singapore, Indonesia
